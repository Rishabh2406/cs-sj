SJ Classes commenced its operations in 2019 with CA Sweta Jain providing Cost and Management Accounting classes to the CA-Inter students.

CA Sweta Jain is a Chartered Accountant and a faculty for Cost and Management Accounting since 2014. She believes in providing conceptual learning to her students and in-depth understanding of the concepts. With more than thousands of students already benefitted from her, she proudly continues to add more students to her teaching league. She has also written her own study material which is in the process of publishing.

At SJ Classes, we believe in building the ability of students to think and understand rather than just “learning by heart”.

  • Classroom like experience
